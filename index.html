I am currently a senior student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), majoring in Computer Science. I will start a new journey as a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of California, Davis in Fall 2020. My advisor will be Professor Cindy Rubio González.

About me

My research interests include Software Engineering, Programming Languages and Machine Learning. I enjoyed using tools from machine learning to work on real problems.

In the second half of 2019, I was a research intern at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) in Saudi Arabia, where I worked with Professor Xiangliang Zhang. We designed a gradient-based algorithm for discrete adversarial attack generation. My first-authored paper has been accepted by KDD 2020.

Prior to that, I worked in Professor Chen Li's group at University of California, Irvine. We worked on a California Wildfire Detection research project collaborated with NASA.

During my undergraduate period at SUSTech, my acdemic supervisor is Professor Bo Tang, with whom I worked on a news headlines generation project.
